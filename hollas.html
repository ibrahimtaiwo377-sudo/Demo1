Welcome to Hollas Eatery Center

Located in Sagamu, Ogun State, Hollas is a well-known eatery center offering a well-equipped and furnished dining experience. Enjoy a variety of delicious dishes, turkeys, drinks, cakes, doughnuts, sausages, and more — all at affordable prices.

Healthy cooking utensils, professional chefs, and fresh plates ensure quality every time.
